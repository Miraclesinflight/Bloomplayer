Privacy Policy

Plain language.
No surprises.

Last updated: April 24, 2026

BloomPlayer is built by a parent, for parents. Your child never creates an account or fills in any form. Everything here applies to you — the parent using the app.

1. Information we collect

a. Personal Information (from parents)
– Email address – required to sign in.
– Name and profile photo – only if you sign in with Google (as provided by Google).
– Parent PIN – stored encrypted in your account.
– Your video library – any YouTube link you save to “My Videos”.
– Watch sessions – which video was watched, how long, and when. This is used only for your Parent Dashboard (watch time, unique videos, progress).
– Child profiles (optional) – you may add a nickname (e.g., “Tiger”) and a broad age range (e.g., “Primary 5–8”). We never ask for a child’s real name, birthdate, or contact information.

b. Non‑Personal Information
– Usage data – anonymized information about how you interact with the app (e.g., number of sessions, device type, age group selected) to help us improve BloomPlayer.

2. How we use your information

– To provide services – sign you in, save your video library, show your Parent Dashboard.
– To improve the app – analyze usage patterns (anonymously) to make BloomPlayer better.
– To communicate – send you sign‑in links or account‑related notifications.
– To ensure security – protect your account and prevent unauthorized access.

We use trusted third‑party services to help run the app (such as cloud hosting, authentication, and analytics). These service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may only use it for the purposes we specify. We do not sell your information to anyone.

3. How we store and protect your information

Storage: Your data is stored securely in the cloud. It is kept as long as your account is active.
Security: We use industry‑standard encryption and security practices. Only you can access your data via your signed‑in account.
Data retention: When you delete your account, your information is removed from our live database within 24 hours. Backups may take up to 30 days to fully cycle out.

4. Your choices and rights

Delete account – In Parent Mode, go to Account → Delete account. This removes your email, PIN, video library, child profiles, and watch sessions from our database within 24 hours.
Export watch log – Download a CSV of your child’s viewing history (for homeschool records) from the Parent Dashboard.
Change PIN – Anytime in Parent Mode.
Access & update – Your email and name are managed through your Google account or the sign‑in method you used.
Manual deletion request – Email hello@bloomplayer.com if you cannot sign in.

5. Children’s privacy

BloomPlayer is a parent‑controlled tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Child profiles store only a nickname and broad age range – no real name, location, or contact details. What you share is entirely up to you; you may use any nickname you wish.
If you believe a child’s information has somehow reached us, email hello@bloomplayer.com and we will delete it immediately.

6. Changes to this policy

If we make a significant change, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We will never quietly add new data collection.
