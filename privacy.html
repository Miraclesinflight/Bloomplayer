Privacy Policy

Plain language.
No surprises.

Last updated: April 24, 2026

BloomPlayer is built by a parent, for parents. Your child never creates an account or fills in any form. Everything in this policy applies to you — the parent setting up and using the app.

What we collect from parents

When you sign in (required to use BloomPlayer), we collect your email address. If you sign in with Google, we also see your name and profile photo (as provided by Google).

You may optionally create child profiles using only a nickname (e.g., “Tiger”) and a broad age range (e.g., “Primary 5–8”). We never ask for a child’s real name, birthdate, or contact information.

Your parent PIN is stored encrypted in your account so it persists across devices.

Your video library & watch activity

Any YouTube link you save to “My Videos” is stored in your account. We also record watch sessions – which video was watched, how long, and when. This is used only to show you the Parent Dashboard (watch time, unique videos, progress over time). You can export your watch log as a CSV from Parent Mode or delete everything by deleting your account.

Third‑party services

BloomPlayer relies on a few trusted services to work. When you use the app, these services may process data as described in their own privacy policies:

• Firebase (Google) – handles sign‑in, data storage (Firestore), and usage analytics.
• Google Analytics – helps us understand basic usage (e.g., number of sessions, device types). We have enabled IP anonymization.
• YouTube – plays videos via embedded iframe. YouTube may set cookies or collect viewing data according to its own policies.
• Ko‑fi – if you click the donation link, you leave BloomPlayer and Ko‑fi’s privacy policy applies. We do not share any data with Ko‑fi.

We never sell your data or use it for advertising.

Children’s privacy (COPPA)

BloomPlayer is a parent‑controlled tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Child profiles store only a nickname and age range – no real name, location, or contact details. If you believe a child’s information has somehow reached us, email hello@bloomplayer.com and we will delete it immediately.

Deleting your account

You can delete your account at any time from Parent Mode → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your email, PIN, video list, child profiles, and all watch sessions from our database within 24 hours. Your Google Analytics data (anonymous) is retained according to Google’s retention policies.

To request manual deletion, email hello@bloomplayer.com.

Data retention

We keep your account data as long as your account is active. Once you delete your account, we remove it from our primary database within 24 hours. Backups may take up to 30 days to cycle out, but the data is not accessible in the live app.

Changes to this policy

If we make a significant change, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We will never quietly add new data collection.

Questions?

Reach us at hello@bloomplayer.com or via the contact page.
