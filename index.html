BloomPlayer is a distraction-free parent tool designed to remove the temptation of endless looping for kids. Created for a loved one on the autism spectrum, it gives families a safe environment to introduce curated videos—helping children watch from start to finish and breaking the habit before it forms.

How it works

No Scrubber Bar

Videos play straight through. The timeline, rewind, and fast-forward buttons are completely hidden.

Parent PIN Lock

Once a video starts, the screen is locked. It requires a 4-digit Parent PIN to close or exit.

Add Your Own

Paste any YouTube link to instantly add it to your child's private, distraction-free library.

A Safe Space

A clean, focused environment designed specifically to prevent stim-looping and encourage healthy watching.

Our Story

I have always wondered — what if he never knew?

Fifteen years of watching. Now a tool for every family starting out — before the habit forms.

My son is 16 and on the autism spectrum. For years, the rewind and forward buttons—that tiny slider at the bottom—became a loop he couldn't escape. He wasn't watching; he was stimming on the same second of video for hours. I often wondered: what if he never knew those options existed?

Ironically, I built BloomPlayer while sitting next to him during one of those loops. It doesn’t "stop" stimming, but it gives us quiet beautiful pockets of time to actually watch something from start to end together.

it’s the tool I needed the day he was diagnosed. But now it’s for every family so no child needs to get lost in a screen loop. It’s about catching the habit before it forms—before they even know there’s another way.

"All these years, I've asked myself the same question: What if the rewind and forward options just didn't exist? What if he never knew it was an option?"
